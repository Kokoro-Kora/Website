    MIS is a major where it involves both technical and business skills. Since we are facing this deadly pandemic (COVID19), a lot of businessese have switched to doing it online, therefore, being a technopreneur is a wise idea.

  • Why MIS

    We are pursuing to study the Management of Information Systems (MIS) because we highly believe that this major allows us to understand a sufficient management behavior with advanced technological demands in the current job market and may arise in the future. We hope to use these technical skills to construct an innovative management infrastructure for solving business problems.
